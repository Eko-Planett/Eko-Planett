Door Decoration

Materials needed:

some straws (already used)

a hot glue gun (you can buy one if you don't already have one- they can come in pretty handy!)

a string (any type is fine)

1. gather up Materials

Find these items in your house and group them all up!

2. Glue the straws

Glue the straws together with the hot glue gun; make them create a square (make it big, so the next step is easier)

3. Cut the straws

Cut the straws to form a shape of your choice, and

You're done!
